About NLN.

What NLN is

NLN is a UK-wide WhatsApp community for T Level students, run by people currently doing T Levels.

Placement help, honest advice, and cross-provider chats grouped by route — Digital, Construction, Health & Science, and every other T Level pathway.

Applications are reviewed manually. Everyone in the community is either doing a T Level, has done one, or is about to start.

Why it exists

T Levels launched in 2020. Six years later there still isn’t one place where T Level students across the UK can actually talk to each other.

Colleges run their own things. WhatsApp chats exist inside single courses. There’s no cross-provider layer — no way for someone doing Digital at one college to find someone doing the same course somewhere else, or ask what a placement looked like at a different provider.

NLN is the first attempt at that. Started in 2026 by Eliud Awuah, a T Level Digital student, because it should exist.
