What this is

NLN is a WhatsApp community for T Level students across the UK. Peer advice, placement help, cross-provider chats.

Who it’s for

Anyone currently doing or about to start a T Level. Year 12, Year 13, or waiting to begin — all welcome.

What happens after you apply

We review each application manually. If it’s a fit, you’ll get a WhatsApp invite link to your email.

Who reviews applications

Eliud Awuah, founder. Currently doing a T Level in Digital.

When to expect a reply

Usually within 48 hours.

A note on safety

Everyone is reviewed. The community has moderation rules shared on entry. You can leave at any time.
