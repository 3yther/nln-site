How it works

  1. Level 01

    Join

    Apply and get vetted. Everyone in the community is doing a T Level, has done one, or is about to start.

  2. Level 02

    Connect

    Meet people on your route across colleges. Ask the questions you can’t ask anywhere else.

  3. Level 03

    Grow

    Placement help, honest advice, and resources written by students who’ve done it.

  4. Level 04

    Level Up

    Opportunities, introductions, and a network that keeps opening doors.

Who’s behind NLN

NLN is an independent student project, run by Eliud Awuah, a T Level Digital student. Started in 2026 because there wasn’t a proper place for T Level students to actually talk to each other across the country.
