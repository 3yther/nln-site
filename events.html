Events.

NLN events are for T Level students, run by T Level students. Small, useful, and cross-provider — the kind of thing that’s hard to find inside any one college. We’re planning three kinds:

Placement Q&As

Small online sessions with T Level students who’ve just finished their industry placement. What worked, what didn’t, and how they got it. Route-specific.

Route meetups

Informal in-person meetups by route, in London and other cities as the community grows. Low pressure. Same-course people in the same room.

Open calls

Occasional wide community calls — new pathway announcements, community updates, whatever’s worth all of us being on together.

What’s scheduled

Nothing yet.

NLN is new. The first events will be scheduled once the community is large enough for them to be worth running — probably by autumn 2026.

If you want to be first to know when they land, apply to join.
