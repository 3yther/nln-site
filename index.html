How it works

    Connect

  4. Level 04

    Level Up

Who NLN is

NLN is an independent student project. It exists because there wasn’t a proper place for T Level students to talk to each other across the country. Not a college forum. Not a careers portal. Somewhere you can ask what a placement is actually like, from someone who did that exact one.

We’re not the DfE, and we’re not IfATE. Nobody here is trying to sell you a course. It’s students on the same routes, comparing notes.
